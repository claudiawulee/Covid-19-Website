Charities

Click any of the boxes below to be directed to the charity's website!

Unicef

Unicef uses donations to deliver life-saving medicine, food, and vaccines to vulnerable children. In addition, they provide essential supplies such as gloves, masks, and access to clean drinking water to families.

United Nations

Donations are used to support organizations under the World Health Organization in order to research more into the virus and future vaccines and treatments. Moreover, they deliver essential supplies to patients and frontline workers.

World Health Organization

WHO is coordinating the global effort in order to help countries with prevention, detection, and response to the pandemic. Donations help with getting supplies, researching, and saving lives.

CDC Foundation

The CDC Foundation raises funds through their Emergency Response Fund which is used to fund state and local health departments, support for the global response, data management, personal protective equipment, critical response supplies and more.
