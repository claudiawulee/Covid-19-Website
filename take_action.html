What YOU can do!

Help others out during these unprecedented times! Keep up with social distancing, donate to charities, advocate for effective legislation to your local government, and educate others around you and through social media about how to slow the spread!

Action Checklist

  • Step 1: Read and understand the safety guidelines especially those in your specific location
  • Step 2: Abide by all the guidelines and encourage others around you to do so as well
  • Step 3: Look into which charities you want to donate to
  • Step 4: Donate!
  • Step 5: Keep up to date with the news about COVID-19
